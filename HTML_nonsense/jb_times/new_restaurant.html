New Restaurant gets
Raving Fans

A new Chinese restaurant has appeared in the city of San Marcos called "The Mandarin". The restaurant specializes in
noodle dishes, and uses interesting sounding names for them. Some names include "Shogun's Delight", "The Panda
Chow-Down", and a very spicy dish simply called "The Dragon". They also serve other dishes such as soups and dumplings.


Mandarin Craze

The Mandarin launched a grand opening where people who came to the restaurant on the day they opened would get 25% off.
As soon as the doors opened, almost all of the seats were already taken up by hungry customers waiting outside. In less than
an hour, people were already lined up waiting for a table to opening up. The restaurant stayed packed and bustling for the
whole rest of the day and most of the evening.

Critical Appraise

With so many people lined up, the food had better be good, right? Luckily, the wait wasn't a waste. As customers came out of
the restaurant, all of them raved about how incredible and flavorful the food was and highly recommended the place. “I
couldn't believe how delicious their Panda noodle dish was,” one customer stated. “Nobody should have to go their life without
coming to The Mandarin.” The restaurant has already gotten 45 5-star reviews in the 1 week that it has been open as of the
writing of this paper, and it continues to rise each day.
